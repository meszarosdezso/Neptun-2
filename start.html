Get started

Requesting a new account

You are getting a new account automatically, with your own Neptun Code once your registration is accepted by the University.

Logging in for the first time

Go to https://login.neptun2.com and click on "I'm new here". Then you'll be asked for your Neptun Code, your student ID, and to generate a new password.

Once you are logged in successfully, you can customize the theme of your own Neptun 2 site.
